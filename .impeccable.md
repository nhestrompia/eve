## Design Context

### Users
EVE is used by developers and product people reviewing completed product changes, implementation evidence, and verification history in local repositories.

### Brand Personality
The interface should feel precise, calm, and accountable. It should support quick inspection without making product history feel decorative or noisy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a restrained product-history UI with clear hierarchy, compact metadata, and pragmatic actions. Keep the existing light theme and avoid visual drama that competes with the record content.

### Design Principles
- Keep product history readable first, with capped line lengths and clear section rhythm.
- Pair product-facing summaries with implementation metadata without making either feel secondary.
- Make primary actions easy to find, but visually tied to the record they affect.
- Preserve full functionality on mobile with stacked layouts and large touch targets.
